How it guides you

Simran, Dhyan, Bhajan. Choose your sitting length; the app divides it into the three parts. Soft bells mark each change — one strike, two, three — so your eyes never need to open.

Bells that never miss. The chimes ring on time even with the screen locked, and the practice closes with three strikes and a gentle Radhaswamy greeting.

Nitya Sumiran. Gentle hourly reminders during the hours you choose — a soft swarmandal note calling you to a minute of remembrance. Silent when your phone is silent.

Presence, not performance. A quiet history of your sittings — golden dots for days remembered, never a scorecard. Backup and restore any time.
